# Cosmocraft-the-game!



  
Cosmocraft is a project for #horizons, it is an exploration game where player can travel through space, land on planets. They can mine, build, and craft of any planet according to their resources. 

**AI was used for functions and domain and names and to make the objects in THREEE.JS. But the logic, maths and features are my own work**



# Why making Cosmocraft ?

- Inspired from minecraft, cosmocraft was made to wnble an experience of exploration, building and crafting out side the panetg earth. 

- It has the correct logic and physics that each planet has, like the gravity on each planet is different, giving a unique experience to players.

- I made this game a i wanted to learn a very important toll called THREE.js, which is used to make 3d animations and elements on web. 

# How to play ?

- Open the link "https://cosmocraft-the-game-fz96r57j1-ayan-s-projects14.vercel.app/"

- Control the player with WSAD, W for moving forward, A for moving left, S for moving backward, and D for moving right.
 
- Press E to get into the spacecraft. Press WSAD to control the spacecraft. Press SPACEA to go up.

- Keep going up and you will escape the earth's atmosphere!!! 

- Explore different planerts by moving towards them, you will see diffrerent planets as you go farther

- **Land on moon(any planet) slowly, if you keep going fasr towards moon, you will surpass its atmospherer and not land**

- Look for the shiny element, and press F to collect the!
